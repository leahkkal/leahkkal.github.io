The Sibley Memorial Hospital Foundation

The Sibley Memorial Hospital Foundation raises money to provide philanthropic support to Sibley Memorial Hospital, a nonprofit, community hospital affiliated with Johns Hopkins Medicine. Contributions to the Foundation help support hospital programs and services, including continuing education for nurses, financial and supportive services for patients, expansion of clinical programs, and research initiatives.

The Foundation is a 501(c)(3) organization and all donations received are fully tax-deductible according to IRS regulations.
